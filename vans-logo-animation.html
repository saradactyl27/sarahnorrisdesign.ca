Vans Logo Animation

This video was a lot of fun to work on. We were task with creating a mock logo animation for a fake or existing company. Vans is a brand that I love and own a lot of, so being able to create something for it was awesome.

I wanted to stay with the street-wear style that vans had so I went with a hand drawn style. Rotoscoping the skateboarder gave the perfect effect to match the style.
