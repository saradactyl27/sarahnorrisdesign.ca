National Arts Centre Poster

This project was one of my favourites to work on. We were tasked with creating a mock poster layout for a past NAC dance performance. This project excited me because it was the first time we were allowed to use typography in an expressive way after learning all our fundamentals.

My vision was to use the typography to mirror the movement of the dancers from the show. After a lot of sketches and planning I was able to create a layout which mirrored the dancer and felt very expressive.

To bring this piece to the next level I decided to create a moving poster with it. By adding motion to the words around the dancer as well as the dancer himself, I was able to increase the dynamic and expressive feel to the poster.
