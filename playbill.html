Shakespeare Playbill

This piece was very satisfying to complete. The task was to create a playbill for a series of plays put on by the Stratford Festival. Each play was to have its own page and each section needed a title page.

Each play has different pieces of information which needed to be given an appropirate hierarchy. This allows the reader to find inforamtion easily and understand what they are reading.
